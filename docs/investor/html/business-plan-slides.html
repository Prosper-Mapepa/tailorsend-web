01Problem

The application gap

  • Students apply to 50–200+ roles per search cycle — each quality application takes 30–60 minutes
  • 98% of Fortune 500 companies use ATS — generic resumes get filtered before a human sees them
  • The tools are fragmented — job boards, ChatGPT, resume builders, trackers — and auto-apply bots submit without review
"I spent more time customizing applications than interviewing."

05Customer

Who pays, and what they pay

Primary customer

  • Michigan college students — seniors & grad students at CMU, U-M, MSU, Wayne State
  • Recent graduates — 20–80 applications in the first 6 months
  • Career changers — retooling for tech, PM, engineering

Usage-based pricing

  • Free — 2 tailor + 1 autofill/mo (funnel)
  • Campus $5 — 8 kits, first purchase
  • Monthly $9.99 / Yearly $89.99 — 25 kits/mo
  • Sprint $29.99 — 50 kits, one-time

Kit = tailor, autofill, or incorporate · COGS ~$0.18/kit · gross margin ~78%
