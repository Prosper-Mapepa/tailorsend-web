1. Executive Summary

The problem

Job seekers manage a fragmented process

Students and recent graduates jump between job boards, resume tools, AI writers, application forms, and trackers. The result is slower applications, inconsistent quality, and missed follow-up.

The solution

One guided workflow from search to follow-up

TailorSend brings job search, honest AI tailoring, ATS scoring, autofill, recruiter outreach, and application tracking into one user-controlled platform.

Search→ Tailor→ Edge→ Autofill→ Outreach→ Track

One "application kit" = one job, start to finish. Live today at tailorsend.cc with Stripe billing.

What makes it unique

🧩The whole job hunt, one tool

Competitors do one slice. TailorSend goes search → tailor → apply → follow-up.

🛡️Honest AI, user in control

Never invents experience. Never auto-submits — the user reviews and sends everything.

📈Proof on every application

Before → after ATS scores show the lift. Priced for students, from $5.

$25,000 One-time ask

Grant / award / seed to bridge Year 1 runway while validating product-market fit and building campus partnerships in Michigan.

Plus SBDC support: market research, tech commercialization, Michigan campus go-to-market, and financial plan review.

4. Target Market

Primary customers

  • Michigan college students — seniors and grad students in campus recruiting (CMU, University of Michigan, MSU, Wayne State, and other MI schools)
  • Recent graduates — applying to 20–80 roles in first 6 months after graduation
  • Career changers — professionals retooling for tech, PM, or engineering roles

Market size (realistic scope)

  • ~300,000+ students enrolled at Michigan public universities
  • ~50,000+ Michigan students job-seeking annually (estimate)
  • Addressable niche Year 1: hundreds of paying users in Michigan, expanding nationally online

6. Marketing & Sales Plan

Year 1 focus: low-cost, Michigan-first organic growth.

  • Campus ambassadors at 2–3 Michigan universities ($0 – small stipends)
  • Posts in student subreddits, Discord, and LinkedIn groups
  • Partnerships with career centers (free workshops: “Tailor your resume in 10 minutes”)
  • SEO content: ATS tips, company-specific resume guides
  • Student .edu pricing to drive word-of-mouth

Marketing budget Year 1: ~$250–400/month (organic only; no paid ads until unit economics proven).
